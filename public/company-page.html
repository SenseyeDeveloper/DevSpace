Про Levi9

Levi9 IT Services Ukraine is known as a professional IT outsourcing company nevertheless being at the cutting edge of technology development.

We truly believe that working at Levi9 is much more than a job — you will be a part of a dynamic, qualified and motivated European team with a unique corporate family spirit.

Levi9 is built on the belief that true innovation happens when you bring together the best minds and ideas for the perfect achievements. That’s what we do.
We’re a fast-growing company made up of vibrant, intelligent, and passionate people, being the employer for specialists at all career levels: from career starters to experienced engineers.

We are aware that Levi9 success is all about the people working here that’s why we aim to attract and retain great talent by ensuring that all of our employees feel intellectually stimulated and supported by attentive talent management.

Still, working at Levi9 Kyiv or Lviv means not only to be challenged but also to have fun! Everyone here works incredibly hard and we make sure to balance this with joyful social activities inside and outside the office.
